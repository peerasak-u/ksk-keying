Allowed values:

usable:
- true: accounting-related and worth keeping, including invoice, receipt, cash bill,
  tax invoice, utility bill, withholding tax certificate, bank statement,
  credit note, debit note, purchase order, quotation, delivery note, or other
  supporting accounting document even if doc_kind is unknown
- false: blank page, cover sheet, unrelated image, advertisement, personal photo,
  unreadable page, obvious duplicate capture, payment-success proof, or page with
  no accounting value

doc_kind:
- handwritten_bill: handwritten cash bill, handwritten receipt, handwritten informal bill
- pwa_bill: การประปา / การประปาส่วนภูมิภาค / Provincial Waterworks Authority bill
- pea_bill: การไฟฟ้า / การไฟฟ้าส่วนภูมิภาค / Provincial Electricity Authority bill
- global_house_invoice: Global House receipt, tax invoice, invoice, or purchase document
- normal_bill_or_invoice: normal printed receipt, bill, invoice, tax invoice, cash receipt, sales receipt
- bank_statement: bank statement, account movement report, passbook page
- wht_certificate: withholding tax certificate / หนังสือรับรองการหักภาษี ณ ที่จ่าย
- credit_note: credit note / ใบลดหนี้ / tax credit note
- debit_note: debit note / ใบเพิ่มหนี้ / tax debit note
- purchase_order: purchase order / ใบสั่งซื้อ
- quotation: quotation / ใบเสนอราคา / estimate
- delivery_note: delivery note / ใบส่งของ / delivery order
- unknown: accounting-related but document kind is unclear

completeness:
- standalone: this page appears complete by itself
- first_page: this page clearly starts a multi-page document and needs the next page
- middle_page: this page clearly depends on both previous and next pages
- last_page: this page completes a previous page
- unknown: cannot tell if complete

confidence:
- high: document kind and completeness are clear
- medium: document kind is likely but some ambiguity remains
- low: hard to read or ambiguous

group:
- income_vat: seller-issued income document with VAT / tax invoice
- income_nonvat: seller-issued income document without VAT
- expense_vat: purchase/expense document with VAT / tax invoice
- expense_nonvat: purchase/expense document without VAT
- bank_statement: bank statement or account movement
- unknown: cannot determine from the page

unusable_kind:
- blank_or_separator: blank page, scanner separator, mostly empty back page
- cover_or_index: cover page, index, folder label, summary sheet without accounting value
- non_accounting_document: personal photo, ad, chat screenshot, unrelated form, non-accounting content
- payment_confirmation: transfer slip, QR payment confirmation, bank app success page, bill-payment success page
- unreadable_or_cutoff: too blurry, too dark, badly cropped, or critical content missing
- duplicate_capture: obvious duplicate photo/capture of another page
- unknown: usable=true, or unusable but the reason category is unclear

Rules:
1. Always fill unusable_kind. Use unknown for usable=true pages.
2. Use this decision order:
   - First decide whether the page is a single primary document or a register/index/summary of many documents.
   - If it is a register/index/summary of many transactions, reject it as
     usable=false, doc_kind=unknown, group=unknown, unusable_kind=cover_or_index.
   - Only choose invoice/receipt/purchase_order/quotation/delivery_note doc kinds
     after you are confident the page is one document, not a list of many.
2. If the page is not accounting-related, set usable=false, doc_kind=unknown,
   completeness=unknown, group=unknown, and choose the best unusable_kind.
3. Payment slips, bank transfer confirmations, QR payment confirmations, and
   bill-payment confirmations are handled by another module. Use
   unusable_kind=payment_confirmation when the page is actual proof that a
   payment already happened, such as a transfer slip, bank app success page,
   payment-success screen, receipt-of-payment screen, or bill-payment
   confirmation. Visible text like จ่ายบิลสำเร็จ, ชำระเงินสำเร็จ, payment
   successful, transfer successful, paid, success, plus a slip-style bank/payment
   app layout, transaction reference, paid timestamp, payer/payee transfer layout,
   or confirmation amount are strong evidence. These should be unusable=false
   even if they prove a real expense payment.
   Do not reject an invoice, bill,
   tax invoice, quotation, purchase order, delivery note, or other accounting
   document only because it shows QR, PromptPay, bank account details, Biller ID,
   payment channel details, or payment instructions.
4. If the page is accounting-related but unsupported, keep usable=true and choose
   the closest doc_kind, or unknown.
5. Internal payment vouchers / ใบสำคัญจ่าย, monthly accounting-office
