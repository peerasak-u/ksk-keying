Extract a delivery note (ใบส่งของ) page, which may be combined with a tax invoice (ใบกำกับภาษี).
Return JSON with top-level key classify matching the required schema.
Use gate.doc_kind exactly as delivery_note.

Rules:
- Copy only visible facts.

- seller = the party that issued the document (labeled ผู้ขาย, ผู้จำหน่าย, ออกโดย, or printed on company letterhead).
  buyer = the party the document is addressed to (labeled ผู้ซื้อ, ลูกค้า, Ship To, ลูกค้า/ผู้รับสินค้า).

- vat_treatment: 7 when the page shows tax invoice markings (ใบกำกับภาษี, receipt/tax invoice, or a printed VAT line with 7%).
  For pure delivery notes without tax invoice markings, use 0 if clearly non-VAT.
  Use null only when VAT status is genuinely unreadable.

- document_no: take from header-level identifiers (เลขที่, ใบส่งของเลขที่, ใบกำกับภาษีเลขที่, Invoice No.).
  Prefer the เลขที่ field. Do NOT use: barcode numbers, tax IDs (13-digit เลขประจำตัวผู้เสียภาษี),
  product codes, or machine/transaction serial numbers.

- seller and buyer tax IDs matter when readable.

- document_date: format as DD/MM/BBBB Thai Buddhist era (e.g. 05/03/2569, not 2026-03-05 and not 05/03/69).
  Take from the header date field (วันที่, Date).

- amounts: extract the printed totals block when visible.
  pre_vat_total = the pre-VAT base (มูลค่าก่อนภาษี, มูลค่าสินค้า, Subtotal, or the sum before ภาษีมูลค่าเพิ่ม).
  vat = the printed VAT amount from the totals block (ภาษีมูลค่าเพิ่ม 7%).
  gross_total = the all-in total (รวมเงินทั้งสิ้น, TOTAL).
  net_payable: always fill this — for delivery notes, net_payable equals gross_total.
  When the page has tax invoice markings (ใบกำกับภาษี / receipt/tax invoice), always extract the full
  VAT breakdown — do not leave pre_vat_total or vat null on a tax invoice.
  When pre_vat_total is not separately printed but gross_total is, and vat_treatment = 7, derive
  pre_vat_total = gross_total / 1.07 and vat = gross_total - pre_vat_total.

- Line items: extract each row from the item table with description, qty, unit, unit_price, and amount.
  Use amount_includes_vat to mark whether the line amount already includes VAT.
  On delivery notes with tax invoice markings, line amounts are typically pre-VAT.
  Include ขนส่ง (delivery charge) rows as separate lines.
  Do not emit the VAT line itself as a line item.

- reference_no: any secondary document reference (เลขที่อ้างอิง, PO No., Ref), if visible.

- If the document number is absent or illegible, set document_no to null — never substitute a number
  from another page, another document, or a report/listing that mentions the same purchase.

- WHT: look for a printed หัก ณ ที่จ่าย line, an attached WHT certificate, or a paid amount cleanly
  lower than the total by 1/2/3/5% of the base. Fill wht only from what the page shows;
  never compute or assume a rate the document does not print.
